Technical Specs

Studio Arsenal

We own, maintain, and configure our filming equipment, lighting rigs, and editing server specs. This gives us full flexibility during high-priority setups.

Digital Cinema Cameras

Arri Alexa Mini LF, RED V-Raptor 8K systems, Sony Venice 2 dual-ISO cinema lines, equipped with anamorphic Cooke and Atlas Orion optics packs.

Lighting Setup Specs

Aputure Electro Storm lines, ARRI Skypanel arrays, Astera Titan tubes, custom DMX projection mapping setups, and high-frequency laser units.

Post Production Server Specs

Multi-seat offline editing rooms, DaVinci Resolve Studio grading desks with full hardware surfaces, and real-time GPU-accelerated motion graphics nodes.
